Risk Scoring

Smart Detection,
Early Intervention

Our risk scoring engine follows the Zambian Ministry of Health Antenatal Care Guidelines and WHO PCPNC framework to ensure every assessment meets the highest medical standards.

Low Risk

Continue routine care with weekly check-ins and educational content.

Medium Risk

Enhanced monitoring with more frequent assessments and targeted advice.

High Risk

Immediate alerts and guidance to seek professional care urgently.
